Privacy Policy

Step Counter - Pedometer
Last updated: June 30, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Step Counter - Pedometer ("we", "our", or "the App") handles information when you use our mobile step-tracking application on Android and iOS.

1. Summary

  • The App is offline-first. Your activity data is stored locally on your device.
  • We do not sell your personal data.
  • We do not require an account to use the App.
  • Health platform sync (Apple Health / Health Connect) is optional and only occurs with your permission.

2. Information We Process

Depending on how you use the App, the following data may be stored on your device:

  • Activity data: step counts, distance, calories, walking time, hourly activity, and activity timeline.
  • Profile information you provide: name (optional), age, height, weight, daily step goal, stride length, and unit preferences.
  • Health logs: weight entries and water intake records you add in the App.
  • App settings: theme, language, notification preferences, and health sync preferences.
  • Health platform data (optional): if you connect Apple Health or Health Connect, the App may read and/or write steps, distance, calories, weight, and height as authorized by you.

3. Permissions

The App may request the following device permissions:

  • Physical activity / motion: required to count steps using your device sensors.
  • Notifications: optional, for goal reminders and motivation alerts.
  • Apple Health / Health Connect: optional, to import or sync health and fitness data you choose to share.

You can revoke permissions at any time in your device Settings. Some features may not work without required permissions.

4. How We Use Information

We use information solely to provide App features, including:

  • displaying your daily steps, goals, statistics, and history;
  • calculating distance, calories, and walking time;
  • generating reports and home screen widgets;
  • syncing with Apple Health or Health Connect when you enable it;
  • creating optional local backups and exports you initiate.

5. Data Storage and Security

  • Primary storage is a local database on your device.
  • Optional encrypted backups may be protected with a device key or passphrase you choose.
  • Encryption keys are stored using platform secure storage (iOS Keychain / Android EncryptedSharedPreferences).

6. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information. Data may leave your device only when you choose to:

  • sync with Apple Health or Health Connect;
  • export or share a report, backup, or file using your device's share sheet;
  • use optional cloud or third-party services if you explicitly enable them in a future version.

7. Data Retention

Data remains on your device until you delete it in the App, clear app data, or uninstall the App. Uninstalling removes locally stored App data from your device.

8. Your Choices and Rights

  • Disconnect Apple Health or Health Connect at any time from App settings.
  • Export your data using Backup & Restore or report export features.
  • Change or delete profile and log data within the App.
  • Control notifications and permissions in device Settings.

Depending on your region, you may have additional rights to access, correct, or delete personal data. Contact us using the details below.

9. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed to children under 13 (or the minimum age required in your country). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

10. International Users

Because data is stored locally on your device, it generally remains under your control on your device. If you use export or sync features, data may be processed by platforms you authorize (for example Apple or Google).

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will update the "Last updated" date above. Continued use of the App after changes means you accept the updated policy.

12. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or your data, contact the app developer / publisher listed on the Google Play or Apple App Store listing for Step Counter - Pedometer.
